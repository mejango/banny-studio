# Banny Studio Privacy Policy

Effective: July 8, 2026

Banny Studio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- Everything you create — shows, audio recordings, images — stays in the
  document files you save, on your device or in your own iCloud Drive.
- The microphone is used only when you record narration or voices onto a
  track, and those recordings are written only to your own project file.
- The app contains no analytics, no tracking, no ads, no accounts, and no
  third-party services. Nothing is ever sent to us or anyone else.

Questions: open an issue at https://github.com/mejango/banny-studio/issues
